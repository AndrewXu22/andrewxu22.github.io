Biography

Hailu Xu is a tenure-track assistant professor in the Department of Computer Engineering & Computer Science at California State University, Long Beach. He received his PhD in Computer Science from Florida International University in Summer 2020. He obtained his master's degree from the Department of EECS at the University of Toledo in Summer 2016 and his bachelor's degree in Computer Science from North China Electric Power University in Summer 2014.

🎓 I am looking for CS/CE students to join our group. Please feel free to send me your CV and unofficial transcript. Students at CSULB can directly visit my office.

Research: System, Data, and AI

Hailu Xu's research focuses on systems with emphasis on AI, Robots, Deep Learning, Big Data, Cloud, and IoT. He designs and implements systems for integrating AI models, human-robot collaboration, supporting efficient distributed deep learning, analyzing social streaming data, handling security issues and recovering failures in streaming systems, and processing data in cloud and edge environments.
