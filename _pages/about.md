I am an **Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Engineering & Computer Science (CECS) at California State University, Long Beach (CSULB)**. My research lies at the intersection of **computer systems, artificial intelligence, big data, cloud/edge computing, and human-centered robotics**. I design **scalable, reliable, and intelligent systems** to support next-generation AI applications and human–robot collaboration.

My long-term goal is to **bridge systems and AI** by building **efficient, trustworthy, and human-in-the-loop computing platforms** for robotics, distributed learning, and cyber-physical systems.
