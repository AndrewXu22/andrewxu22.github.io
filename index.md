## Biography

Hailu Xu is a tenure-track Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Engineering & Computer Science at California State University Long Beach. He received his Ph.D. in Computer Science from Florida International University in 2020.

His research focuses on systems with emphasis on AI, robotics, deep learning, big data, cloud computing, and IoT.

- Distributed Deep Learning
